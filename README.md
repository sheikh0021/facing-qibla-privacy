# Privacy Policy for Facing Qibla

Last updated: June 14, 2026

## What this app does with your location

Facing Qibla uses your phone's location to calculate the direction from where you are to the Kaaba in Makkah. This calculation happens entirely on your phone. Your location is never sent to any server, never stored anywhere, and never shared with anyone.

## Permissions this app uses

Location permission (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION and ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION):
This is used only to calculate which direction you should face for prayer. The app does not record, log, or transmit your location.

## Data we do NOT collect

- We do not collect personal information
- We do not have analytics or tracking
- We do not show ads
- We do not have a server that receives data from your phone

## Children

This app does not collect any information from anyone, including children.
